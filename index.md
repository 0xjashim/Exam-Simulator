## Features

<div class="feature-grid">
  <div class="feature-card">
    <div class="icon">📄</div>
    <h3>UPLOAD ANY DUMP</h3>
    <p>Load .txt or .md dump files. Correct answers auto-detected from bold, ★ prefix, or [CORRECT] tag.</p>
  </div>
  <div class="feature-card">
    <div class="icon">💾</div>
    <h3>SAVE & RESUME</h3>
    <p>Save progress as a .json file mid-exam and resume exactly where you stopped — any time.</p>
  </div>
  <div class="feature-card">
    <div class="icon">📊</div>
    <h3>EXPORT REPORTS</h3>
    <p>Download results as Excel (.xlsx) or CSV with per-question breakdown and score summary.</p>
  </div>
  <div class="feature-card">
    <div class="icon">🔀</div>
    <h3>SHUFFLE MODE</h3>
    <p>Optional shuffle checkbox so you study the content, not the sequence.</p>
  </div>
  <div class="feature-card">
    <div class="icon">✅</div>
    <h3>INSTANT CHECKING</h3>
    <p>Check each answer immediately with wrong answers in red and correct answer revealed in green.</p>
  </div>
  <div class="feature-card">
    <div class="icon">🔒</div>
    <h3>FULLY OFFLINE</h3>
    <p>Zero backend. Files read into browser RAM only. Nothing sent to any server — ever.</p>
  </div>
</div>

## How to Use

<div class="terminal">
  <div class="terminal-bar">
    <div class="sim-dots">
      <span class="sim-dot r"></span><span class="sim-dot y"></span><span class="sim-dot g"></span>
    </div>
    <span style="margin-left:6px;">instructions.sh</span>
  </div>
  <div class="terminal-body">
    <div><span class="prompt">$</span> <span class="cmd"># Step 1 — Prepare your dump file</span></div>
    <div class="out">Save Word dump as .txt — bold answers auto-convert to **bold**</div>
    <div><span class="prompt">$</span> <span class="cmd"># Step 2 — Launch the simulator</span></div>
    <div class="out">Click ▶ Launch Simulator above or visit /simulator</div>
    <div><span class="prompt">$</span> <span class="cmd"># Step 3 — Upload and start</span></div>
    <div class="out">Drop your .txt file, optionally enable shuffle, hit ▶ Start Exam</div>
    <div><span class="prompt">$</span> <span class="cmd"># Step 4 — Save progress anytime</span></div>
    <div class="out">Click 💾 Save Progress → .json file → upload later to resume</div>
    <div><span class="prompt">$</span> <span class="cmd"># Step 5 — Export results</span></div>
    <div class="out">Finish exam → download Excel or CSV session report</div>
  </div>
</div>
